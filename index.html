About Pops Bitrock

Built on Bitrock with near zero gas fees and super fast speed, Pops is a utility token which will act as the foundation of our ecosystem, to provide rewards and further advantages to our holders and community alike. With POPs we pay homage to the much beloved childhood fan favourite "Regular Show", in which Pops sacrificed himself for the good of his friends and family. Lets all be a little more like Pops!

Taxes

Taxes actively feed both the LP (liquidity pool) and MW (marketing wallet).

All funds accumulated within the marketing wallet shall be utilised to yield farm on multiple chains, bringing further liquidity back onto the Bitrock Ecosystem and shall be managed by the founder and his team.

The yield generated from our strategies will fund future BBBs (buyback & burns), smart contract and website development and so much more!

Tax System

Taxes will be used for liquidity and development purposes

Standard Taxes: 10/10 Buy/Sell Tax (04% Liq, 16% Marketing) Anti-Jeet Tax (48hrs): 05/25 Buy/Sell Tax (05% Liq, 25% Marketing) Anti-Jeet Tax will slowly decay over a number of days until reaching the standard taxation rates as mentioned above
